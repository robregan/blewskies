What is Holistic Massage?

The human body is a complex and interconnected system.  Blew Skies Holistic Massage follows the understanding that the physical, spiritual, and emotional components of a human being are equally important. When discomforts in the body arise, holistic massage treats the whole body not just the areas of concern.
